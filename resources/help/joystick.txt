Joystick Module

The Joystick component represents a standard analog module with 2 axes (X and Y) and an integrated push button (SW).

Pins:
- GND: Connect to Ground (0V).
- 5V: Connect to VCC (+5V).
- X: Analog output for X-axis (voltage varies from GND to VCC).
- Y: Analog output for Y-axis (voltage varies from GND to VCC).
- SW: Digital output for the integrated button. Connects to GND when pressed.

Operation:
- Drag the joystick handle with the Left Mouse Button to change X and Y values.
- The handle will return to the center (500, 500) when released.
- The Red color on the handle indicates that the button is being pressed during the drag.

Properties:
- Resistance: Total resistance of the internal potentiometers.
- Pos_X: Current X position (0-1000).
- Pos_Y: Current Y position (0-1000).
- Pressed: Current button state.
